WHAT IS NEO?

NEO IS China's
first original public chain

NEO, former name—Antshares, is China's first ever original and open source public chain project, which was founded in 2014
and launched on Github in June, 2015. Ever since it was founded, NEO’s team has experienced many ups and downs in the
blockchain industry as well as digital currency market, and the government’s changeable/unclear policies. Nevertheless, we
believe that technology is the force that motivates the development of the world. Pushing forward by the tide, we will step into
a new era of “smart economy.”

THE
TECHNOLOGY
BEHIND NEO

Through technologies such as P2P network, dBFT consensus, digital certificate,
Superconducting Transactions and cross-chain interoperability, NEO blockchain enables one
to manage smart assets in a fast, efficient, safe and certified manner.

First Original Blockchain in China

Founded in 2014, NEO became open
source on GitHub in 2015. NEO is
the innovator and leading force in
numerous new blockchain
technologies. It is also the leading
public blockchain in China.

Underlying design supports
various digital assets

NEO’s Underlying design supports various
digital assets. One can register, spread,
trade and circulate assets all by oneself.

Digital Certificates solves problems
of trust

NEO supports digital certificates, solves the
potential problem of untrustworthy nodes on
a public blockchain. With digital certificates,
one can legally issue assets on the blockchain
and be protected by the regulations

Original Superconducting
Transactions mechanism

This mechanism makes a trustless digital
exchange possible. One can order match
various types of digital assets without the
need to top-up.

Turing complete Smart Contracts

NEO smart contracts are Turing complete,
executes in the NEO VM and has
advantages including determinability, being
terminable, resource controllability, parallel
execution, ability to partition and limitless
performance scalability.

Smart Contracts in high level programming languages

Supporting languages such as C#,
Java, Go, developers can promptly
start developing smart contracts
on NEO blockchain.

NEO VM lightweight Virtual
Stack Machines

NEO VM has short booting time and
high execution efficiency, coupled with
‘certainty call tree’ technology, it can
deliver limitless scalability.

Innovative dBFT consensus mechanism

Consensus nodes use Byzantine Fault
Tolerance Algorithm to reach consensus and
ensure the finality of transactions. It is also
ensured that the system keeps its finality and
operability as longa as less than 1/3 of the
nodes are observing dBFT errors.

Innovative Cross-chain Operation Protocol

comprising of Cross-chain Asset Swap Protocol
and Cross-chain Distributed Operation protocol,
can achieve atomic asset swap and
guarantee the operation consistency of smart
contracts executed on different chains.

Lattice cryptology-quantum computer-proof technology

Use Lattice-based cryptograph
signature and encryption technology,
which can prevent the quantum crisis as
Lattice-based cryptography provides
encryption problems (e.g. SVP) beyond
the capability of quantum computers.
