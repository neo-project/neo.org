About the Event

    Neo Smart Economy is excited to announce the first NEO Developers Conference which will take place on January 30-31, in San Francisco.

    With multiple sessions and workshops NEO DevCon is bringing the NEO community together for a 2-day conference full of innovative ideas and projects, featuring many top speakers from the Blockchain space.

    Come and join us to learn about the existing and upcoming NEO based projects, meet leading developers from different parts of the world and become a member of the global NEO community！
